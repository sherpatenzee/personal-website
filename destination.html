Everest Base Camp Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel.
  • Day 2: Fly from Kathmandu to Lukla (2,800m/9,186ft) and start the trek to Phakding (2,652m/8,700ft).
  • Day 3: Trek from Phakding to Namche Bazaar (3,440m/11,283ft).
  • Day 4: Acclimatization day in Namche Bazaar. Hike to the Everest View Hotel or visit the Sherpa Museum.
  • Day 5: Trek from Namche Bazaar to Tengboche (3,870m/12,694ft). Visit the Tengboche Monastery.
  • Day 6: Trek from Tengboche to Dingboche (4,410m/14,468ft).
  • Day 7: Acclimatization day in Dingboche. Hike to Nagarjun Hill or visit the Chhukung Valley.
  • Day 8: Trek from Dingboche to Lobuche (4,910m/16,109ft).
  • Day 9: Trek from Lobuche to Gorak Shep (5,170m/16,961ft) and visit the Everest Base Camp (5,364m/17,594ft).
  • Day 10: Hike to Kala Patthar (5,643m/18,513ft) and back to Gorak Shep.
  • Day 11: Trek from Gorak Shep to Pheriche (4,371m/14,342ft).
  • Day 12: Trek from Pheriche to Namche Bazaar.
  • Day 13: Trek from Namche Bazaar to Lukla.
  • Day 14: Fly from Lukla to Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel.

This itinerary can vary depending on the specific route and pace of the trek. It's important to allow for adequate acclimatization and rest days to avoid altitude sickness. Our experienced guides will take care of all the details and ensure that your trek is safe and enjoyable.

Annapurna Base Camp Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel.
  • Day 2: Drive/Fly from Kathmandu to Pokhara (820m/2,690ft).
  • Day 3: Drive from Pokhara to Nayapul (1,010m/3,313ft) and start the trek to Tikhedhunga (1,570m/5,151ft)..
  • Day 4: Trek from Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani (2,850m/9,348ft).
  • Day 5: Hike to Poon Hill (3,210m/10,529ft) for sunrise and then trek to Tadapani (2,630m/8,628ft)..
  • Day 6: Trek from Tadapani to Chomrong (2,170m/7,119ft).
  • Day 7: Trek from Chomrong to Dovan (2,600m/8,530ft).
  • Day 8: Trek from Dovan to Deurali (3,230m/10,595ft).
  • Day 9: Trek from Deurali to Annapurna Base Camp (4,130m/13,550ft)..
  • Day 10: Trek from Annapurna Base Camp to Bamboo (2,310m/7,579ft).
  • Day 11: Trek from Bamboo to Jhinu Danda (1,780m/5,840ft) and visit the Jhinu Hot Springs.
  • Day 12: Trek from Jhinu Danda to Pothana (1,960m/6,430ft).
  • Day 13: Trek from Pothana to Phedi (1,110m/3,640ft) and drive back to Pokhara.
  • Day 14: Drive/Fly from Pokhara to Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel.

This itinerary can vary depending on the specific route and pace of the trek. It's important to allow for adequate rest days to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Our experienced guides will take care of all the details and ensure that your trek is safe and memorable.

Langtang Valley Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu and transfer to your hotel.
  • Day 2: Day 2: Drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi (1,550m/5,085ft).
  • Day 3: Trek from Syabrubesi to Langtang village (3,430m/11,253ft).
  • Day 4: Trek from Langtang village to Kyangjin Gompa (3,870m/12,694ft).
  • Day 5: Acclimatization day in Kyangjin Gompa. Hike to Tserko Ri (4,985m/16,368ft) or visit the Langtang Lirung Glacier.
  • Day 6: Trek from Kyangjin Gompa to Ghora Tabela (3,080m/10,104ft).
  • Day 7: Trek from Ghora Tabela to Tarke Ghyang (2,590m/8,497ft).
  • Day 8: Trek from Tarke Ghyang to Sermathang (2,610m/8,563ft).
  • Day 9: Trek from Sermathang to Melamchi Pul Bazar (870m/2,854ft) and drive back to Kathmandu.

This itinerary can vary depending on the specific route and pace of the trek. It's important to allow for adequate rest days to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Our experienced guides will take care of all the details and ensure that your trek is safe and memorable.
